Blip

Developed for the GeoJam 2021, Blip was my first creation that actually made it out into the world. This week long game jam encouraged me to focus on my passion for game design and development.

The game managed to come 7th overall from player voting, features a leaderboard stored online, a colour based level making system and inspired me to pursue further game creations.
